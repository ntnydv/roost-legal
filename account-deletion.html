Account Deletion

How to delete your account and what happens to your data.

ðŸ“‹ How to Request Account Deletion

You can request permanent deletion of your Roost Inspect account and associated data by following either method below:

Option 1: Via Email

  1. Send an email to contact@my-roost.com with the subject line:
    Account Deletion Request
  2. Include in your email: the email address associated with your Roost Inspect account so we can locate and verify your identity.
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days and begin processing the deletion.

Option 2: In-App

  1. Open the Roost Inspect app and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  3. Confirm your decision when prompted. You may be asked to re-authenticate for security.

In limited circumstances, certain data may be retained after your account is deleted:

Data Type Reason for Retention
Approved inspection records Finalised inspection reports that have been shared with and approved by other parties (e.g., landlords or property managers) may be retained for those parties' legal and record-keeping obligations. Your personal identifying information will be minimised in retained records.
Legal compliance records Minimal records may be retained as required by applicable law, regulation, or to resolve ongoing disputes.
Anonymised analytics Aggregated, anonymised usage statistics that cannot identify you may be retained for service improvement purposes.
Active subscriptions: Deleting your Roost Inspect account does not automatically cancel any active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store before requesting account deletion to avoid further charges.

Stage Timeframe Details
Confirmation Within 2 business days We confirm receipt of your deletion request via email
Account Deactivation Within 7 days Your account is deactivated and can no longer be used to log in
Data Deletion Within 90 days All personal data is permanently removed from our systems and backups
Grace period: During the first 7 days, you may contact us to cancel your deletion request and restore your account. After this period, deletion is irreversible.

â„¹ï¸ Important Notes

  • Irreversible: Once the 90-day deletion period is complete, your data cannot be recovered. Please ensure you export any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.
  • Shared inspections: If you have shared inspections with other parties, those parties may retain copies of finalised reports for their own records.
  • App store subscriptions: Account deletion does not cancel subscriptions managed by Apple or Google. You must cancel these separately.
  • Re-registration: You are welcome to create a new account after deletion, but your previous data will not be available.
