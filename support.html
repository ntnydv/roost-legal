How do I start a new property inspection?
  1. Open the Roost Inspect app and tap "New Inspection" on the home screen.
  2. Enter the property address and select the inspection type (e.g., move-in, move-out, routine).
  3. Optionally, upload a lease document â€” our AI will automatically parse the details and pre-fill property information.
  4. Follow the guided room-by-room walkthrough to capture photos and notes for each area.
  5. The app uses your device's compass and accelerometer to guide consistent, comprehensive photo capture.
How do I invite other parties to an inspection?

Roost Inspect supports multi-party collaboration, so all relevant parties can review and contribute to the same inspection:

  1. Open the inspection you'd like to share.
  2. Tap the "Invite" or "Share" button.
  3. Enter the email address or phone number of the person you want to invite (e.g., your landlord, tenant, or property manager).
  4. Choose their role and permission level.
  5. They'll receive an invitation to view or collaborate on the inspection. If they don't have Roost Inspect, they'll be prompted to download it.
How do I export or download an inspection report?

Once your inspection is complete:

  1. Open the completed inspection from your inspection list.
  2. Tap "Generate Report" or the PDF icon.
  3. The app will create a comprehensive PDF report with all photos, annotations, and details.
  4. You can then download, share, or email the PDF directly from the app.

Reports include timestamped photos, room-by-room condition notes, and any annotations added during the inspection.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion at any time. For full details on the process, what data gets deleted, and the timeline, please visit our dedicated Account Deletion page.

In short: email contact@my-roost.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", and your data will be permanently removed within 90 days.

What happens to my data when I upload a lease?

When you upload a lease document, the text content is sent to OpenAI's API for parsing. This extracts key details like tenant names, property address, lease dates, and other relevant information to pre-fill your inspection.

The lease document is stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage. OpenAI does not retain your data after processing. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • We use Firebase's robust security rules to restrict data access
  • Biometric authentication data is processed entirely on your device and never sent to our servers
  • We do not sell your data or display ads

Read our full Privacy Policy for comprehensive details.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

An internet connection is required for most features, including syncing data, uploading photos, parsing lease documents, and generating reports. Some basic features may work offline with data syncing once you reconnect.

How do credits and subscriptions work?

Roost Inspect may offer credits or subscription plans to access premium features. All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. You can manage your subscription through your app store account settings.

For refund requests, please contact the respective app store directly.
